**Project Title: Telco Customer Churn Dashboard.**

AUTHOR: M.HAMZA KHAN

**Problem Statement:** The goal is to analyze customer behavior to predict and prevent churn.

Data Source: Sample Data Sets (Kaggle).

**Key Findings:**

High Risk: Month-to-month contract holders have a churn rate of ~42.7%.

Retention Factor: Customers with "Tech Support" and "Online Security" are much more likely to stay.

Tenure: New customers (1-6 months) are at the highest risk of leaving.
